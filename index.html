Investment structure

Flexible entry points designed for both first-time international investors and seasoned portfolio builders.

Matix Estate offers a tiered approach to entry capital, allowing you to select the level that best aligns with your strategy—whether you’re targeting a single seaside pied-à-terre or a small cluster of units across multiple cities.

Each range reflects a typical total project budget, including acquisition, design, furnishing, and operational setup. Before you commit, we work through expected rental performance and usage so the apartment fits both your financial targets and lifestyle expectations.
